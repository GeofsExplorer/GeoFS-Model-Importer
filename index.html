‚ú® Features

  • Easy Model Placement: Place GLTF/GLB models as **static objects** on the ground at your current aircraft's location.
  • Aircraft Replacement: Use your custom model to **replace the active aircraft model**, which tracks its position and orientation (heading, pitch, roll).
  • Dynamic Scaling: Easily adjust the **scale** (default 1:1) of the imported models using the intuitive UI controls.
  • Center of Mass Marker: Toggle a visible **Center of Mass (CoM) marker** for placed models to aid in positioning.
  • Clean Slate: Automatically removes previously imported models when placing a new one.

üìù How to Use

  1. **Open GeoFS:** Navigate to the game, and once loaded, click the **"Model Importer"** button at the bottom left to open the control panel.
  2. **Set Scale:** Adjust the **Scale slider** or input a value (e.g., 1.0) to set your desired model size.
  3. **Select Model:** Click the file input and choose your **GLTF (.gltf) or GLB (.glb)** file.
  4. **Action:** Choose one of the following:
    • Click **"Place Here"** to add the model as a static object.
    • Click **"Use as Aircraft"** to replace your current plane's visual model.

**Note:** Placing a new model or replacing the aircraft will remove any model previously imported using this tool.
